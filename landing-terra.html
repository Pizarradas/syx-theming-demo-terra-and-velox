Our Approach

A better food system, built from the ground up

Every decision we make starts with a simple question: is this good for the planet, the farmer, and you?

🌾

Verified Local Farms

All 320+ partner farms are within 150km of your city. Every farmer is verified in person — no industrial producers, ever.

📦

Zero-Waste Packaging

100% compostable boxes, reusable glass jars, and a return scheme with 94% participation. Packaging you’re proud to receive.

💚

Fair Farm Premium

Farmers keep 78% of each sale — vs. 42% in conventional retail. We believe the people who feed us should earn a living wage.

🗓️

Flexible Subscriptions

Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly — with full customisation. Pause, skip, or cancel anytime with a single tap.

🍲

Seasonal Recipe Cards

Every box includes chef-designed recipe cards using exactly what’s in your delivery — inspiration, not obligation.

🌍

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

Our last-mile fleet is 100% electric. We offset the remaining emissions through reforestation projects with certified partners.
